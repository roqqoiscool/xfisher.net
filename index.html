WHAT IS XFISHER?
xFisher is a student-led initiative revolutionizing fishing with a user-friendly device featuring remote operation, depth control, and automatic jigging. With a focus on affordability and simplicity, we aim to fill a gap in the market. Our active presence on social media platforms promotes visibility. The team operates with clear roles, ensuring efficient progress. Sustainability is integral to our product, offering eco-friendly charging options. Through this project, we've gained valuable skills and maintain structured accounting for financial transparency. Our immediate goal is to develop a top-quality product, followed by targeted marketing efforts to attract buyers.
ABOUT US!
xFisher is a student-run company revolutionizing the fishing industry by offering a unique "box" with a movable arm that simplifies fishing. With advanced features such as depth control, automatic jigging, and adjustable speed, we provide an affordable and user-friendly solution. Through dedicated teamwork and a focus on sustainable development, we aim to enhance the fishing experience for everyone. Visit our social media channels for more information and join us on the journey towards a better fishing world.
