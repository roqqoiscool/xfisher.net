ABOUT US!
xFisher is an innovative student company dedicated to simplifying the fishing industry and enhancing the fishing experience. We are passionate about developing practical solutions that make fishing more accessible and efficient for individuals.

Our groundbreaking idea involves the development of a unique "box" equipped with a movable arm that can be controlled with a remote. This box is designed with advanced features that allow users to control the depth of the hook, perform automatic jigging, adjust the speed, and control the movement of the arm.

Market research has shown that there is limited availability of similar products in the market, and those that exist are often expensive and inaccessible to ordinary consumers. Therefore, we are committed to offering a product that is affordable and easy to use, while maintaining high quality and functionality.

We are committed to enhancing our visibility and recognition in the market, and we have strategies in place to market ourselves on social media and websites. Through platforms like TikTok, YouTube, and Twitter, we aim to engage with our audience and share our product with the world.
